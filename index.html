Speaks your kitchen

Malay terms, explained as you cook.

Alex never assumes you know the vocabulary — and never dumbs it down either.

Rempah
spice paste

The blended base of chilies, shallots, garlic, and aromatics fried until fragrant before anything else goes in.

Santan
coconut milk

Added late and on low heat — Alex will warn you before it's poured in, since it splits easily if rushed.

Pecah Minyak
oil separation

The visual cue that a rempah is properly cooked — oil visibly pulls away from the paste.

Belacan
shrimp paste

Pungent and essential to many sambals. Alex offers substitutes if it's not available locally.
